LeBron James Career Highlights

  • NBA Debut: October 29, 2003 (Cleveland Cavaliers)
  • Teams Played For:
    • Cleveland Cavaliers (2003–2010, 2014–2018)
    • Miami Heat (2010–2014)
    • Los Angeles Lakers (2018–present)
  • Championships: 4 NBA Titles (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • All-Star Appearances: 20 (and counting!)
  • NBA MVP Awards: 4 (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
  • Career Points: Over 40,000 – the most in NBA history
  • Olympic Gold Medals: 2 (2008, 2012)
